Secondhand Swap and Fashion Show

About The Event

Join us for a unique Secondhand Fashion Show & Clothing Swap, where sustainability meets styling! Watch our models walk the runway in secondhand clothing styled by our team. Before or after the show, swap your gently used clothing for new gently used pieces, refreshing your wardrobe in an eco-friendly way.
